How to Automate Competitor Reward Scraping

The ProblemThe AI Solution
Manually checking competitor sites (like Rakuten) for cash-back rates is tedious, and the team no longer has resources for it. Use NotebookLM as an "AI scraper." By "sourcing" it with the live content of competitor sites, you can get this data in seconds.
Primary Roles: Account Directors, Account Managers Tools: NotebookLM, Google Chrome Difficulty / Phase: Super Easy Win

Step-by-Step Training Workflow

Step 1: Set Up Your "Competitor Notebook"

In NotebookLM, create a new Notebook named "Competitor Reward Rates."

Step 2: "Source" the Competitor Sites

  1. Open a Chrome tab and go to the competitor's page (e.g., rakuten.com).
  2. In NotebookLM, click "+" to add a source and select "Website URL." Paste in the URL.
  3. Workaround: If the URL is blocked, "Select All" (Ctrl+A) on the competitor's page, "Copy" (Ctrl+C), and paste it into a "Text" source in NotebookLM. Title it "Rakuten Data - [Today's Date]".

Step 3: Ask NotebookLM for the Data

Now you can ask questions based *only* on the data you provided.

Based on all the sourced competitor data:
1.  What is the cash-back rate for [Merchant Name] on Rakuten?
2.  What is the rate for [Merchant Name] on TopCashBack?
3.  Are there any "special offers" mentioned for them?

Step 4: Update Your Sources (The "Refresh")

This is your new, 2-minute "bi-weekly report." Just delete the old sources, paste in the new text from the live sites, and ask the same questions.
